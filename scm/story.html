Our Story

Company Overview and Strategy

Sthenic Capital Management Limited is a Nigerian registered business with a focus on investment management. The company offer services in specialized financing, investing and trading to clientele which span our network, partners and ecosystem. The company is well known for its strong risk management tactics driven by value-added approaches. It is led by a team with a deep understanding of the local investment terrain and strong capabilities for structuring private debt.

Our Strategy

The cornerstones of our strategy include execution strength, agility, and speed. Our platform helps us achieve this by bringing together established investors looking for alphas and growth firms looking for investments that unlock high returns to build value.
