Overview

Who We Are

Sthenic Capital Management Limited (SCM) is Sthenic’s investment management and regulated subsidiary, with a focus on structured private debt fund management. SCM is a specialized private debt fund management company that provides tailored financing solutions for mid-market companies. Our team of experienced professionals brings a deep understanding of the markets we operate in, enabling us to identify and unlock unique investment opportunities. We work closely with our clients to provide flexible financing solutions that meet their specific needs, helping them achieve their growth objectives.

Pedigree

  • Proven depth of experience and expertise in debt and equity capital raise from both the local and foreign markets

  • Over ten years of multi assets and multi-sector research capabilities and deep dive involvement.

  • Over fifty live special situation investing and fiducial businesses under management.

Platform and Personnel

  • Sthenic is built on a strong network of focus segment partners and related ecosystems and is supported with strong advisory services.

  • Intentionally trained and cultured investment advisory team members.

  • Strategically located office in Dolphin Estate Ikoyi, Lagos with a team of 14 highly skilled analysts.

Our Strategy
