Who We Are

Sthenic Group is a full-fledged financial advisory and investment management firm dedicated to helping businesses and investors achieve financial success through sustainable and value-added investments.Founded in 2016 and incorporated in Lagos, Nigeria, our company is authorized to offer a broad range of high-quality financial services, spanning structured finance, financial advisory, fund management, equity capital raise, mergers & acquisitions, and custodian services. We focus on providing customized solutions that help clients build a strong foundation for the present and a clear vision for the future, making us a fast-growing leader in the industry.
