Who We Are

Sthenic Finance and Advisory Limited (“Sthenic”) is a structured finance and investment management company with special focus on supporting growth business at resolving short and medium-term liquidity challenges through innovative financing products and structures. Our strategy is built on the strength, agility and speed of our execution. We bring together entrenched investors seeking alpha and growth companies in need of capital that unlock high returns and create increased value for all stakeholders. Founded in 2016 and incorporated in Lagos, Nigeria. Over the years, the Sthenic group has successfully evolved into a finance, business advisory, and investment management business. Within our business ecosystem, we are licensed by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC) as a Fund Manager and operates specialized Funds that simplify financing, value add and risk management. We focus on providing customized solutions that help clients build a strong foundation for the present and a clear vision for the future, making us a fast-growing player in the industry. We help businesses in Africa achieve financial sustainability and growth through smart capital. We are dedicated to filling the missing middle in the continent.
