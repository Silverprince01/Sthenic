Our Story

At Sthenic Capital Management, we understand that the path to greatness often starts with humble beginnings. That is why we are proud to say that our f irm was incorporated during the Covid year in 2020. Despite the challenges posed by the pandemic, we believed that resilience and adaptability are qualities for any successful investor, and we have demonstrated both in our approach to managing capital. Our f irm was built on an existing and robust advisory ecosystem, with a clear mandate to grow investment into profitable returns for our investors and provide financing to small and mid-sized businesses. And We have displayed these mandate across different firms and industries in our portfolio. We're proud to continually serve the missing middle. Over the past three years of operation, our Sthenic Special Situation Debt Fund has financed over 20 businesses with 63 specific investments, with cumulative investments of NGN1.7bn over time. We are committed to making a meaningful impact in the markets we serve, and we believe that the opportunities in the African market remain unmatched. We see increasing growth in wealth and savings among the middle class across emerging markets; we can channel this wealth from surplus hands to underserved markets to lead to significant gains in impact investing and economic expansion. At Sthenic Capital Management, we are here to ride on the opportunities provided by the markets we serve to make a pivotal shift for the small and mid-sized markets.

Our Strategy

The cornerstones of our strategy include execution strength, agility, and speed. Our platform helps us achieve this by bringing together established investors looking for alphas and growth firms looking for investments that unlock high returns to build value.
