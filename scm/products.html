Our Products

Asset Backed Note

This debt note is extended for the purpose of providing support in the expansion of business operations, through the acquisition of assets. Structuring of this credit, is a function of the cashflow of the assets, and associated risk and is available to businesses seeking to finance cash flow generating or receivable backed asset, at low covenants.

Purchase Order Financing Note

This financing option can be structured as a line of credit secured by the company’s active Purchase Orders (PO). It is available as fallback to businesses with low cash flow reserve due to long receivable days, for the execution of the PO.

Working Capital Financing(WCF) Note

Our WCF offers business owners a breather in financing, the variable part of working capital, to only cover liquidity for production expansion and execution for targeted customers. This is to be linked directly to revenue and approved invoices from qualified customers and can be financed with senior debt or mezzanine debt. WCF could be structured as a line of credit and secured by the cash flow of the business at the top-line, with repayment recognized as a first line charge item, on the cashflow waterfall.

Receivable Line Of Credit Note [Loc]

A receivable line of credit is available to businesses with stable contractual production offtake, that can be scaled. The facility is backed by cashf low from pledged invoices, and you pay interest on amount borrowed.

Inventory Financing Note

This debt note provides liquidity against inventory of a business, particularly financing the purchase of raw materials required for production of inventory. It is available for merchandising businesses, with need to hold stock for established customers and can be structured for the inventory to be owned by the investor. The business will be a direct off take of produced inventory, on a take-or-pay arrangement and a warehousing arrangement will be required to ensure management of collateral.

Invoice Discounting Note

Invoice discounting allows a business to leverage the value of its account receivables (invoice) to access financing for the business. The invoice will be pledged as the collateral. The amount issued on this facility is less than the total amount of outstanding receivables.

Venture Capital Financing Note

Our Venture Capital Financing provides business growth capital to early stage and later stage startups and is intended to propel a product’s success or growth in the marketplace. This however does not always involve the disbursement of credit facility; it can be provided in form of technical or managerial expertise.

Export and Trade Financing Note

This debt note facilitates trade and commerce, making it possible and easier for importers/exporters to transact through trade.Trade finance supports companies to trade either domestically or internationally. This can be structured, to the individual transactions under the following, invoice factoring, account receivable factoring, consignment purchases.

Leverage Buy-out Note

The LBO note supports businesses in the acquisition of a profitable target company with growth opportunities and can be financed using debt instrument. The asset of the company being acquired, and the acquiring company is used as a collateral for facility. Often asset backed, LBO is available to businesses who desire to acquire another company, as a means to diversify their portfolio.

Project Financing Note

Project finance is available to fund major project and structuring the finance around the project backed by an off take or purchase agreement as the primary source of repayment. The facility structure relies primarily on the project’s cash flow for repayment, with the project assets, rights and interest held as secondary collateral.

On-Lending Financing Note

This facility is available to intermediaries, who offer financing to its customers or projects, to create value. This note could be structured as direct investment to the financial institution of their customer and is backed by the cashflow of the financial institution.

Eco-System Financing Note

Eco system financing leverages on relationship to unlock value for businesses in the value chain or ecosystem. This financing arrangement integrates financial services into a non-financial value chain, thereby unlocking long term opportunities in these businesses.
